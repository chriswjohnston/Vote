What I'm trying to build

A more open conversation with residents.

One of the problems in Nipissing Township is that residents too often have to chase information after the fact. I want this campaign — and how I would serve on Council — to work the other way around.

Listen better

Make it easier to reach out

Residents should not feel like they have to know the system to be heard.

Explain more

Communicate clearly

Public issues should be easier to follow, easier to understand, and easier to respond to.

Follow through

Respect the message

Good communication is not just receiving feedback. It is showing that it was heard and taken seriously.
