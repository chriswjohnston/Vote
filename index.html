A Better Direction

Nipissing deserves better records, better communication, and better leadership.

The Need for Change

The problem is not a lack of decisions. It's a lack of oversight.

Across hiring, procurement, strategic planning, and implementation, the same pattern keeps showing up: processes drift, the public is left behind, and Council does not visibly correct course.

01

Lack of Transparency

Important decisions and supporting documents are too hard to find, follow, and understand.

02

Weak Oversight

When projects drift or timelines slip, residents should be able to see who asked questions and who corrected course.

03

Poor Communication

Residents should not have to hunt through scattered pages, PDFs, and streams just to understand what's happening.

The Platform

Six Commitments. One Direction.

A stronger public record, clearer communication, and real accountability — built as practical improvements, not expensive slogans. Behind each commitment are specific motions, resolutions, and by-laws ready for day one.

View Full Platform The Action Plan
Point 01

A Complete, Searchable Public Record

Meeting pages, searchable documents, by-laws, resolutions, and summaries residents can actually use.

Point 02

Clear, Consistent Communication

Residents should not have to hunt for updates. Township information should reach them in a predictable way.

Point 03

Transparency in Decisions

Recorded votes and a clearer decision trail so people can see what was decided and by whom.
